Privacy Policy

At Hot Beans, the security of your personal information is a foundational priority. We recognize that when you share your details via our online forms whether you are applying for a career or reaching out with an inquiry you are placing your trust in us. We take that responsibility seriously.

At Hot Beans, your privacy is never for sale. We maintain a strict policy of not sharing your data with third parties, ensuring that your personal information remains within our secure ecosystem and is never traded or used for external marketing purposes.
